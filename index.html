Introduction

Healthy eating is about consuming a balanced diet that provides your body with essential nutrients. This includes a variety of foods in the right proportions to maintain health and energy levels.

Benefits of Healthy Eating

A healthy diet helps maintain a healthy weight, reduce the risk of chronic diseases, and promote overall well-being.

Healthy Foods

Here is a list of some healthy foods and their nutritional benefits:
